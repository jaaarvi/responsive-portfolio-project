About Jes

artist, educator, now designer

Contact Me

A daughter of photographers and self-taught artist, Jes has always held a passion for art and design with simple yet impactful identity.

Dabbling in photography, art, and graphic design as a young student, the import of art and design in our our daily lives has always been apparent. Working in museum education at a world-class art institution only heightened the sense of importance in art and in the human response to it.

Jes brings these artistic and professional experiences to bear in their design philosophy; balancing aesthetic with usability.

Each design must be usable but also a joy to use.
